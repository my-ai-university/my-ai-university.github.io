Paper Abstract

This research explores the potential of AI-powered teaching assistants to enhance the learning experience in modern classrooms. We examine the key challenges in deploying AI-driven systems, highlight best practices for integration into existing curricula, and discuss the ethical and pedagogical implications of AI as a tool for both educators and students.
